Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Cloud Motion Lab LLC ("we," "our," or "us") built Minesweeper: Classic Mine Puzzle ("the App") as a commercial application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require an account, login, or registration. Game statistics, scores, and preferences are stored only on your device and are never sent to any server.

Camera and Microphone

The App does not access your camera, microphone, photos, contacts, or location.

Advertising

The App displays optional rewarded ads provided by Unity Ads. No forced or interstitial ads are shown. The App uses Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework to request consent before any ad-related tracking occurs. If you decline tracking, ads are still shown but are not personalized. Unity Ads may collect non-personal technical data (such as device model and OS version) to serve ads. For more information, see Unity's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases, including a weekly subscription and a lifetime access option, processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. All purchase management, including refunds and cancellations, is handled through your Apple ID account settings.

Data Storage

All app data, including game statistics, best times, streak data, sprint records, theme preferences, and purchase status, is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Daily Challenge

The Daily Challenge feature generates a puzzle using a date-based seed so all players receive the same board. No personal data is collected or shared to enable this feature. Your completion status and times are stored locally on your device only.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages and does not contain age-restricted content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Unity Ads — for displaying optional rewarded advertisements
Apple StoreKit — for processing in-app purchases
Apple Game Center — for leaderboards (optional)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
